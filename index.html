Departments

The Cardiology Department is dedicated to providing comprehensive care for heart-related conditions. Our team of expert cardiologists uses the latest technology to diagnose, treat, and prevent a wide range of cardiovascular diseases. We aim to offer personalized care that focuses on both treatment and prevention of heart conditions.

The Emergency Department is the front line of the hospital, operating 24/7 to manage a wide range of medical emergencies. The primary goal is to stabilize, diagnose, and treat patients, or refer them to appropriate departments for further care.

The Diagnostic Department is responsible for performing various tests and imaging procedures to assess a patient’s condition. It supports nearly every clinical department, helping in early detection, disease management, and prevention.

The Neurology Department in a hospital specializes in diagnosing, treating, and managing disorders related to the nervous system, which includes the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves. Services

Pharmacy services in a hospital play a critical role in patient care by managing the safe and effective use of medications. These services include various functions such as dispensing medications, providing drug-related information, monitoring patient drug therapies, and ensuring compliance with medication policies.

Hospital laboratory services play a crucial role in the overall healthcare system by providing the necessary diagnostic, monitoring, and preventive information to medical professionals. These services encompass a wide range of tests and analyses on bodily fluids, tissues, and other samples, which are essential for diagnosing diseases, guiding treatment plans, and monitoring patients' health progress.
